Who We Are

We are a large group of dreamers who believe everyone should have their own lovely space in the universe made to fit their own tastes. Established back in 10067, we have been making worlds real for over 5 decades, and we don't plan on stopping anytime soon!

What We Do

We make dreams come true with the creation of custom worlds. Pick terrain, atmosphere, flora and fauna to your liking. Nothing is out of the question. Our scientists can even create and clone custom creatures for you. With WorldCo you can truly make your world your own.
